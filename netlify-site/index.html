Pet Village · PR #51 · v1.4.0

Two new park maps & the Bump mini-game

Mini-games are moved out of the town square into two walkable park maps — West Green and East Green — connected by new east/west paths off the central plaza. A new competitive mini-game, Bump, rounds out the roster.

What's new

🗺️ Two new park maps

  • West Green — Skip Rope booth + Bump arena
  • East Green — Paper Toss arcade
  • South path → shore (Fishing) is unchanged
  • Parks are compact tiled maps with grass, paths, and prop depth sorting matching the town style
  • Building the same map twice was deduplicated into src/systems/parkMap.ts — a shared map builder both parks use

🎮 Bump mini-game

Walk into the West Green arena and talk to the Bump booth to start. Pick your difficulty:

DifficultyTaps/sec to winEnergy costCoins on win
Easy ~4 taps/sec 5 energy 8 coins
Medium ~6 taps/sec 8 energy 18 coins
Hard ~8 taps/sec 12 energy 32 coins

How it works:

  1. Pick your difficulty and pay the energy upfront
  2. A random villager (MINITEEN or NPC) is chosen as your opponent
  3. Stare-down phase — hold still; tapping now costs energy with no effect
  4. Mash phase — tap / click / mash as fast as you can to fill your meter; the bottom tug bar shows who's winning in real time
  5. When your meter fills, you shove the opponent — if they're below the threshold, they topple off and you win
  6. Win → coins awarded. Lose → no coins, energy already spent

🔋 Energy gates on all mini-games

Every mini-game — Bump, Skip Rope, Paper Toss, Fishing — now checks State.data.pet.energy < 5 before starting. If energy is too low, the booth / dock / replay button shows a short message and refuses to launch. No more soft-locking the pet in an exhausted state.
