Fishing got teeth: surge reels & bait thieves

Shore casting still rewards long throws with rarer fish — but the fight is no longer a hold-forever button mash. Hard fish surge, and deep water sometimes steals your bait.

TL;DR
  • Surge reel — hold during the calm, ease off while it thrashes. Near/easy fish stay mostly hold-through.
  • Bait steal — longer casts can fake a bite; hooking (or missing) ends with “Bait stolen!”
  • Status line calls thrash vs calm so you can time the pump.

What changed

  • Reel update uses a per-hook surge cycle (faster on tougher fish).
  • Holding through a thrash spikes tension; releasing during thrash cools the line.
  • Wait phase rolls a steal chance (~4% short cast → ~20% max cast).
  • Decoy nibbles look like real bites until you set the hook.
  • App version bump to 1.2.1.

How to try it

  1. Open Shore → start Fishing.
  2. Short cast near the dock — easy common fish should still land on mostly continuous holds.
  3. Max-power cast out deep — watch status flip between Calm — hold and Thrashing — ease off.
  4. Keep casting far until you see a nibble that resolves to bait stolen (no inventory fish).
