Pet Village

Controls

Walk, care for Mochi, decorate your house, and earn coins.

Play the game

Inputs

Input Action
W A S D / arrows Walk around
Click / tap Walk to that spot (Club Penguin style)
E / click Interact when close (door, shop, arcade, your pet)
I In house: open the decorate menu
Click furniture In house: pick it up into inventory
Drag + release Paper toss: slingshot the paper ball
ESC / click outside Close a menu

Village tips

  • Mochi follows you everywhere. Food / Happy / Energy decay in real time — including while the game is closed (capped at 12h). Feed snacks, play, and tuck Mochi into a bed to restore energy.
  • Bella's Shop sells food and furniture for coins.
  • Paper Toss earns coins: 10 throws per round, 3 coins per basket, +2 streak bonus from 3 in a row. Wind changes every throw and the bin wanders.
  • Your house is a decoratable grid: buy furniture, place with I, click to pick it back up.
